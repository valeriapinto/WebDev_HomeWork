Monochromatic Series

This Instagram series was created with the intention to challenge my eye for color. I wanted to find places and moments where the colors in a scene were similar, to create a monochrome effect. The result was unexpected - stormy skies, architecture, street art (@phoebenewyork @ethanarmenart), museum exhibitions
(Dia Beacon @danflavin) and secret hallways. It all comes together by contrast of color.
